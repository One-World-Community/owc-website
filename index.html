Our Mission

1. Create inclusive spaces where people can connect over shared interests.
2. Bridge the gap between online and offline communities.
3. Empower individuals to explore and pursue their passions.
4. Build a global network of local communities.

The Technology

1. Cut through the noise and find content you are interested in via fully open RSS feeds.
2. Find other like minded people on the platform and host / join relevant events.
3. Post your own content (you own your content, you own your data).
4. We allow you to setup a jekyll blog that you fully own and control in one click.
